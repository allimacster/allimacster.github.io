Common questions

How do I delete my account?

Email us at support@markovalabs.com with the subject line "Delete my account" and we'll take care of it within 48 hours.

How do I report a user or listing?

Use the report button inside the app on any listing or profile. Our moderation team reviews all reports promptly.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at support@markovalabs.com with a description of the issue and what device you're using. Screenshots help a lot.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. You can read our full Privacy Policy for details.

I have a partnership or press inquiry.

Reach out to alli.markovic@markovalabs.com and we'll respond as soon as possible.
